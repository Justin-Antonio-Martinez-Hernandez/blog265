Our Unforgettable Trip to Argentina 🇦🇷❄️

📍 Bariloche, Río Negro

By: Justin, Alyson, Leila, Arturo, Eugenio & Ximena

The Journey Begins

Last winter, my team and I decided to visit Bariloche, Argentina. It was an amazing experience! We traveled by plane and arrived at the city early in the morning.

Exploring the Landscapes

During the trip, we saw the most beautiful landscapes; the mountains looked like something from a movie. We walked through the town and bought famous local chocolates. One afternoon, we went to Cerro Catedral and tried skiing for the first time. Some of us fell down, but we laughed a lot!

Food and Memories

We also took a boat tour across the Nahuel Huapi Lake. The water felt freezing, but the view was worth it. At night, we ate traditional Argentine steak and drank some local wine.

We loved this trip because we shared special moments and learned about a new culture. It was the best adventure we had together!
